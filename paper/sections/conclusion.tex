\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We presented \textsc{anneal}, a framework for statistically rigorous autonomous
artifact optimization that addresses three gaps in prior LLM-guided optimization
systems: lack of statistical significance testing, absence of structured search,
and no mechanism for accumulating knowledge across optimization runs.

\textsc{anneal} introduces \NUM{16} enhancements organized across four tracks.
The Representation track structures the optimization context to reduce
prompt-following variance.
The Search track provides dual-agent mutation, two-phase refinement, and
island populations for systematic exploration.
The Knowledge track uses episodic memory and lineage context to prevent
re-exploration of failed directions.
The Evaluation track applies Wilcoxon signed-rank testing with Holm-Bonferroni
correction to make statistically defensible accept/reject decisions.
